Project 50 ~ Hot Air Balloon

storyline:
-3 kids going to grandparents' house in hot air balloon
-collecting fruit and gifts for grandparents
-on the way, birds and planes can hit hot air balloon

scoring:
-3 lives total
-birds and planes = obstacles
-if touching obstacle = life -1
-if collecting fruit and gifts = score +5

background:
-series of images in loop

animations:
-hot air balloon --> up, down, right, left
-obstacles --> bird/plane flying (random positions)
-balloon pops when touching obstacles
